My motorcycles

I have 2 motorcycles. An Aprilia RS4 125, which was my first motorcycle, and my latest motorcycle, the Ducati Monster S2R 800. I have been riding motorcycles for about 5 years now. I have a lot of fun riding them and I also enjoy working on them. They are my way of relaxing and getting away from the stress of everyday life. I have been to a few motorcycle events, like theKonnuslahti, 79150 moottoripyörä messut and I am planning to go to more in the future. I plan on getting a larger motorcycle this year, but Im not sure what to get yet. I dont really know why I started motorcycling, but it has quickly become one of my favorite hobbies. I have done quite a few roadtrips with my motorcycles, the longest one being about 1000km all together. I plan on doing a roadtrip this summer maybe to the west coast of Finland.
